Davido, Rema, Top AFRIMMA Nominees List

Life & Style |

By Vanessa Obioha 

The African Muzik Magazine Awards (AFRIMMA) has revealed its list of nominees for 2023, with Davido taking the lead with six nominations. The acclaimed ‘Unavailable’ artist is a contender in categories such as AFRIMMA Album of the Year, Song of the Year, Artist of the Year, Crossing Boundaries with Music, Best Collaboration, and Best Male Artist West Africa.

Following closely are Rema and Burna Boy, each securing three nominations, including recognition in the Artist of the Year and Crossing Boundaries with Music categories.

Tiwa Savage, Odumodu Black, and Omah have each earned two nominations.

This year marks the prestigious awards’ 10th anniversary, promising a gathering of stars from across Africa. The AFRIMMA 2023 Award Show is scheduled to take place at the Meyerson Symphony Center in Dallas, Texas, on September 17. See the full list of  nominees below.
