Nobody wins by playing defense, you score on the other side and the way to score is for Nigeria to have in my view, an export-oriented industrial manufacturing stance. That export-oriented industrial manufacturing stance is like a funnel, in which you bring in more forex because you are developing your value chains that are export-oriented and earning a lot of forex, as opposed to a redistributive model of a small amount of forex. That is a fundamental structural change. That means that the country has to build industrial value chains in which it has a huge competitive advantage.
